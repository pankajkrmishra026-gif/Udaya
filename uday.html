Uday Mondal is an English grammar educator and author dedicated to helping students learn English easily and independently. His book Practical English Grammar has been specially designed for learners under the WBBSE and WBCHSE boards, particularly those living in rural and remote areas of West Bengal who may not have access to proper English learning environments.

"Education should be available to all — regardless of geography, background, or financial circumstance."

Understanding the challenges faced by financially disadvantaged students, Uday Mondal created this self-learning grammar guide so that learners can study English without the need for private tuition. His goal is to make English learning simple, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone.

Through clear explanations and practical lessons, his book helps students build a strong foundation in English grammar and improve their confidence in using the language. The book also serves as a helpful guide for teachers, guardians, and self-learners who wish to strengthen their knowledge of English.

Uday Mondal believes that education should be available to all, and his work reflects a strong commitment to supporting students in their journey to learn English effectively and independently.

Uday Mondal – Author & English Grammar Educator

Uday Mondal is an English grammar author and educator committed to making English learning simple, practical, and accessible for students from all backgrounds. He is the author of Practical English Grammar, a self-learning guide specially designed for students of WBBSE and WBCHSE, particularly those living in rural and remote areas of West Bengal.

With a deep understanding of the challenges faced by learners who lack access to quality English education and private tutoring, he developed this book to help students learn independently with clarity and confidence. His teaching approach focuses on building a strong grammatical foundation through easy explanations, practical examples, and learner-friendly methods.

Uday Mondal's work reflects his dedication to supporting students, teachers, and self-learners in improving their English skills. His mission is to promote accessible education and empower learners to achieve success through self-study and consistent practice.
